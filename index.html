SAY YES TO HEALTHY EATING


Choosing what you eat very important because it leads to great health benefits. Firstly, from eating healthy food, you can reduce the risk of getting many illness such as heart disease, stroke, diabetes, bone loss, cancer, anemia, and many more. Additionally, eating right food will help you reduce high blood pressure and lower cholesterol. When you choose to eat healthy food, you need to choose food with nutrients such as vitamins, minerals, protein, carbohydrates, fats, and lots of water.

Nowadays, people are obsessed with the concept of living fast and with the help from new technology in food industries, convenience food emerged as the new trend of food, satisfying the desire of people demanding for simplicity. As a result, fast food restaurants can be found everywhere and they are dominating the food service industry.
